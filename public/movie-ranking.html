The Best Movies According to me

My top movies of 2026 so far:

Masters Of The Universe

Really enjoyable movie. Great adventure with an 80's vibe. So funny.

Project Hail Mary

A scientist ends up in space and gets help from an unlikely friend.

Super Mario Galaxy

If you love the games you'll love the movie.

The Sheep Detectives

A shepherd is found dead and his flock of sheep try and work out who did it.
